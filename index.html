Terms & Conditions

1. Introduction

By using this website, you agree to these Terms and Conditions. This platform provides digital top-up services for BGMI UC.

2. Services Provided

We facilitate the transfer of digital in-game currency (UC) to the provided Player UID. It is the user's responsibility to enter the correct UID.

3. Payment

All transactions are processed securely via UPI. We do not store your credit card or banking details.

4. User Responsibilities

You must ensure the accuracy of the Player ID (UID) provided. Transactions processed to incorrect UIDs due to user error cannot be reversed.

Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect your BGMI Player ID (UID) and In-Game Name (IGN) solely for the purpose of delivering the digital product. We process payments via UPI and do not store financial data.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for order fulfillment, customer support, and transaction verification.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent user data to third parties. Information is only shared with our payment gateway (UPI) as required to process your transaction.

Refund & Cancellation

1. Digital Goods Policy

Because the products we sell are strictly digital in-game currency, all sales are final. Once the UC is successfully transferred to the provided UID, no refunds or cancellations can be processed.

2. Failed Transactions

If your payment is deducted but the order fails on our end, a full refund will be automatically initiated to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.

3. Incorrect UID

We are not responsible for UC sent to an incorrect UID provided by the user. No refunds will be issued in such cases.

Shipping & Delivery

1. Delivery Method

All deliveries are purely digital. No physical product will be shipped to your address.

2. Delivery Time

UC is typically credited to your account instantly or within 10-15 minutes. In case of server delays, please allow up to 24 hours for the transaction to reflect in your BGMI account.
